Estate Planning Checklist
Tick each arrangement you currently have in place.
CPF Nomination Not Done
Directs CPF monies (OA, SA, MA, RA) upon death. CPF is NOT covered by your Will — must be nominated separately. Without nomination, funds are distributed under the Intestate Succession Act via the Public Trustee.
Will Not Done
Legally specifies how your non-CPF assets are distributed. Without a Will, the Intestate Succession Act applies and may not honour your intended wishes for your loved ones.
Trust Not Done
Assets held by a trustee for named beneficiaries. Ideal for minors, special needs beneficiaries, or multi-generational wealth. Can bypass probate and provide structured, conditional distribution.
Lasting Power of Attorney (LPA) Not Done
Authorises a donee to manage your personal welfare and/or finances if you lose mental capacity. Must be registered with the OPG, Singapore. Without one, a Court order may be required — costly and time-consuming.
Insurance Policy Nomination Not Done
Names beneficiaries directly on life policies under the Insurance Act (s73 / s49L). A statutory trust nomination protects proceeds from creditors and bypasses your estate for faster direct payout.
